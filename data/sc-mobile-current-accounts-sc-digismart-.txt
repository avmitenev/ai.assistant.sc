## SC DigiSmart - Standard Chartered Kenya

Designed to meet all your banking needs

### SC DigiSmart Account

Enjoy this fully digital account uniquely designed to give you the convenience and ease of transacting digitally via the comfort of your mobile phone at an affordable monthly fee.

*   ![Xtrasaver benfit istock ](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/XtraSaver_Benfit_iStock-510409050.jpg)

    ### FREE Debit Card

    Your FREE and secure Visa Debit card will allow you make online purchases and pay for goods & services at Point of sale (POS) globally.

*   ![Person, Human, Face](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/ke-convenience-benefit-list-pintile-400x400px.png)

    ### Enjoy convenience

    Save money and enjoy peace of mind when you send unlimited telegraphic transfers, set up standing orders between standard chartered accounts and local SCB ATM Withdrawals for FREE.

*   ![Person, Human, Glasses](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/ke-e-statements-digi-benefit-list-pintile-400x400px.png)

    ### Zero Salary Processing Fee

    No charge for salary processing. Additionally get SMS notifications when you get salary credits into your account.

*   ![Mobile Phone, Cell Phone, Electronics](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/ke-digital-banking-benefit-list-pintile-400x400px.png)

    ### FREE Digital Banking

    Enjoy convenient banking anytime anywhere with mobile banking and online banking. Transfer funds between accounts, buy airtime, manage your account and your personal details.

**Key Features and Charges**

Non-SCB ATM cash withdrawal Charges: KES 150

Local Transfer Between SCB Accounts: FREE

Local Transfer from SCB to Local Banks (Pesalink): [As per Service & Price Guide](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/docs/ke-stanchart-tariff-guide.pdf)

Online Local Transfer from SCB to Local Banks (RTGS/EFT): FREE

Online International Transfer: FREE

Online Banking access: FREE

Mobile Banking access: FREE

Monthly eStatements: FREE

Other charges: [As per Service & Price Guide](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/docs/ke-stanchart-tariff-guide.pdf)

Non-SCB ATM cash withdrawal Charges: KES 150

### Eligibility & Documents

#### Eligibility

*   Nationality: Kenyan citizen / Resident
*   Age requirement: Minimum 18 years old


#### Mandatory supporting documents for Kenyan citizens and Non Kenyan residents

*   Original National ID or valid passport
*   SC Mobile: Selfie / Portrait In-Branch: One passport size photograph
*   If US Person, upload W-8 BEN / W-9 BEN form.
*   If US Green Card holder, upload W-9 BEN.
