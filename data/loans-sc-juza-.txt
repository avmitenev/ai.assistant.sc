**Weza Na SC Juza**

SC Juza mobile loan app is the solution you’ve been waiting for. Borrow from Kes 1,000 and pay interest for only the days you have the loan. Download today.

**How it works**

* Download the app
* Registration Fast and easy registration process
* Create your PIN Create your own personalized PIN, keep this number safe so that you can login securely to the SC Juza App.
* Apply for a loan Enter how much money you need. Enjoy fair and transparent costs with a longer tenor of 61 days!
* Repayment Simple repayment process. Repay on time to build a positive credit history.

**High loan limits**

With SC Juza you can borrow from KES 1000 up to KES 100,000 every week.

**Flexible repayments**

With SC Juza you have up to 61 days to pay the loan back. That’s not all you choose when to pay because you only pay interest for the days you have the loan. You can pay in full or choose to pay in bits.

**Mini Statements**

With SC Juza you can track your transactions at the click of button. You can view your mini statements conveniently whenever you want.

**No hidden charges**

With SC Juza the power to manage the cost of the loan is in your hands. SC Juza will show you your total loan amount and the cost of the loan giving you confidence to achieve your goal

**Competitive interest rate**

You only pay interest for the days you have the loan.

**Easy to Apply**

SC Juza is designed to make it easy for you to apply for the loan and track your repayment.

**SC Juza Loan Features**

* Interest rate 1.60% pm
* Processing fee 5.50%
* Maximum loan period 61 days

An additional service fee of 5.5% is payable in the second month for loans that have not been repaid within the first 30 days.
