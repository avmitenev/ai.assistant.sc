## How to do a pin reset for credit or debit card 

**How to do a pin reset for credit or debit card**

**STEP 1**

Login to the App

**STEP 2**

Select “Service Requests”

**STEP 3**

Go to “Card Management”

Select “Change Debit Card PIN” if changing debit card PIN

Select “Change Credit Card PIN” if changing credit card PIN

**STEP 4**

Select the appropriate card

**STEP 5**

Enter and re-enter you new PIN

**STEP 6**

Input OTP and continue to change PIN
