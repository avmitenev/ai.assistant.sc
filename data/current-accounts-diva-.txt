## Diva Current Account | Exclusively for Women

Enjoy this account uniquely designed for women that gives you the convenience and ease of managing all your financial transactions for just one monthly fee.

### Enjoy convenience

Save money and enjoy peace of mind when you send unlimited telegraphic transfers, set up standing orders between standard chartered accounts and local SCB ATM Withdrawals for FREE.

### FREE Digital Banking

Enjoy convenient banking anytime anywhere with mobile banking and online banking. Transfer funds between accounts, buy airtime, manage your account and your personal details.

### FREE Cheque Book & eStatement

Enjoy your FREE Cheque Book if you desire one and keep up to date on your account via FREE eStatements.

### FREE Debit Card

Your FREE and secure Visa Debit card will allow you to access cash globally at any VISA enabled ATM, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can also use it for online purchases and also pay for goods & services at Point of sale (POS) globally.
