## How to move money from Mpesa to bank account

How to move money from Mpesa to bank account

**STEP 1**

Go to “Lipa na Mpesa” on your phone

**STEP 2**

Select “Pay Bill”

**STEP 3**

Enter Business number as 329329

**STEP 4**

Input your StanChart Bank account number

**STEP 5**

Input amount

**STEP 6**

Input your Mpesa PIN and confirm
