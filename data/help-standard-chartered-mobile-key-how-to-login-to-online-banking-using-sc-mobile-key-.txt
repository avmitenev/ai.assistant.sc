## How to login to Online Banking using SC Mobile Key

How to login to Online Banking using SC Mobile Key?

**STEP 1**

Login with your username and password

**STEP 2**

You will be prompted that a notification is sent to your SC Mobile App

**STEP 3**

Approve / Reject the Ibanking login on the App. A notification will appear on your mobile

**STEP 4**

You are now logged into Ibanking
