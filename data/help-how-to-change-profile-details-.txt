## How to change profile details, e.g. email address

**How to change profile details, e.g. email address**

**STEP 1**

Login to the App

**STEP 2**

Select “Service Requests”

**STEP 3**

Go to “Personal Details” > “Update Personal Details”

**STEP 4**

Input OTP and submit

**STEP 5**

Change required field(s) and proceed

**STEP 6**

Confirm changes and proceed

**STEP 7**

Enter OTP and submit to complete
