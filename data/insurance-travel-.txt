### FAQs

*   Who Qualifies for the Product?
    
    Any Standard Chartered Bank individual current account holder.
    
*   What modes of Payment are available?
    
    Buy this product via the SC Mobile app in minutes and pay from any of your SCB current accounts or credit cards
    
*   Why should you purchase your Travel cover through us?
    
    • Convenience of your Travel cover issuances  
    • One-stop shop for your insurance solutions  
    • Review and negotiations on various travel insurance products  
    • Follow up for speedy facilitation/feedback/settlement of claims by the insurance service provider
    
*   What happens after I have bought my travel insurance?
    
    You will immediately receive your policy documents by e-mail. You will also receive a confirmation sms from Standard Chartered Bank and from Sanlam Insurance
    
*   What is covered in Travel Insurance Policy?
    
    • Emergency Medical and Related Expenses  
    • Pre-Existing Emergency Medical and Related Expenses for business & leisure comprehensive  
    • Accidental Death and Permanent Total Disablement  
    • International Journey Cancellation, curtailment or Extension  
    • Personal Liability  
    • Hijack & Hostage or Wrongful Detention  
    • Luggage Delay or Loss of luggage
    

*   How many cover options do we have on the Travel Insurance?
    
    • Business Administrative Plan  
    • Leisure Comprehensive Plan  
    • Schengen Plan  
    • Incoming Plan
    
*   Can I purchase a travel insurance for my family?
    
    You can currently purchase under the Leisure and Incoming Travel plans for your family. The coverage per single policy is limited to 2 adults and a maximum of 4 children.
    
*   What is a Business Insurance Plan?
    
    Business Insurance Plan is an enhanced Travel Insurance Plan for individuals travelling from Kenya to other countries for the purpose of business. The scope of cover for this plan is worldwide but excluding the Sanctioned Countries.
    
*   What is Leisure Insurance Plan?
    
    Leisure Insurance Plan is an enhanced Travel Insurance Plan for individuals travelling from Kenya to other countries outside the country for the purpose of having fun or leisure. The scope of cover for this plan is worldwide but excluding the Sanctioned Countries.
    
*   What is a Schengen Insurance Plan?
    
    The Schengen Plan is a Travel Insurance Plan for individuals travelling within the Schengen Countries
    
*   What are the Schengen countries?
    
    The Schengen countries comprises of European states that have officially abolished passport and all other types of border control at their mutual borders. They include Austria, Belgium, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, and Switzerland.
    
*   What is an Incoming Insurance Plan?
    
    The Incoming insurance plan covers travellers from other countries who are travelling into Kenya.
    
*   Do these Travel Insurance plans have an age limit?
    
    Yes, all the insurance plans currently are limited to persons between the ages of 0 to 69 years.
    
*   What documentations are required for purchase of the Travel Insurance?
    
    You will be required to have a copy of your Immigration passport to be able to purchase our Travel Insurance.
    
*   Whom do I contact in case of a claim?
    
    Get in touch with Sanlam General ,Standard Chartered’s 24 hour client contact centre or the Service Provider captured on the policy(Medical Services Organization) in case of medical assistance whilst out of the country.
    
*   What does the family include in coverage?
    
    The family rates include cover for you, your travel companion and dependant Children(up to 4 in number)
    
*   What do I do should I require an Embassy VISA letter?
    
    Please get in touch with Sanlam General Kenya Limited via the contacts scb@sanlam.co.ke or 0719035148
    
*   Who is a child under the travel insurance?
    
    A child refers to a dependant under the age of 18 years and share cover free of charge with their parents.
    
*   What documentation would be required in the event of loss of luggage, cash or documents?
    
    A written police or airline report is required for the checked in luggage.
    
*   Are pre-existing illnesses covered?
    
    Yes its covered under the Business and Leisure plan but limited to inpatient medical expenses only.
    
*   Are there exclusions under the Travel Insurance Policy?
    
    Yes there are as captured on the policy wording provided by Sanlam.
    
*   Whom have we partnered with for this Travel Insurance Product?
    
    We have partnered with Sanlam General Insurance Company Ltd who have partnered with Medical Services Organization as their Global Partner.
