## How to change your SC Mobile Key PIN?

**STEP 1**

Login to the App & click on “Settings”.

**STEP 2**

Select “SC Mobile Key”.

**STEP 3**

Click “Change PIN for SC Mobile Key”.

**STEP 4**

Enter Old PIN.

**STEP 5**

Create new PIN.

**STEP 6**

Confirm new PIN.

**STEP 7**

PIN has successfully changed.
