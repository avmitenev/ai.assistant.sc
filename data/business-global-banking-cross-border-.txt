    ### Dedicated Relationship Manager
    
    You have a dedicated manager to manage your interests and oversee your total banking relationship. One dedicated Relationship Manager will be your overall key point of contact.
    
*   ![Image 3: Operating account business working capital](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/Operating-Account-Business-Working-Capital.jpg)
    
    ### Global Coverage, local access
    
    Straight2Bank lets you enjoy seamless access to all your accounts with us, anytime, anywhere, across multiple countries.
    
*   ![Image 4: Extensive collateral ke](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/Extensive-collateral-ke.jpg)
    
    ### Extensive Credit & Collateral Coverage
    
    Easy access to credit facilities across all three countries. Use of one collateral in your domicile country to secure lending (in local currency or USD) to finance your business across the region. Discuss this further with your Relationship Manager.
    
*   ![Image 5: Global coverage local access chinese business banking](https://av.sc.com/ke/content/images/Global-Coverage-local-access-Chinese-Business-Banking.jpg)
    
    ### Access to financial transactions in Kenya and Uganda
    
    • Cash withdrawals in either local currency or USD (Max USD 20,000 or local currency equivalent per day) • Cash deposits in local currency or USD (Max USD 30,000 equivalent per day) • Balance enquiries • Statement requests – charged as per tariff.
