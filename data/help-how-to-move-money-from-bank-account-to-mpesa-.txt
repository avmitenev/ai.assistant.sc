How to move money from bank account to Mpesa

STEP 1

Login to the App

STEP 2

Go to “Payments” and select either “Pay to Saved Biller” or “One Time Bill Payment”

STEP 3

If doing One Time Bill Payment

Select “Mobile Money” > “Mpesa” and enter the mobile number

STEP 4

Select account to transfer from

STEP 5

Input amount and description

STEP 6

Continue

STEP 7

Confirm details and continue

STEP 8

Input OTP and confirm
