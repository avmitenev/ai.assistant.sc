## Eligibility & Documents

#### Eligibility

*   Nationality: Kenyan citizen / Resident
    
*   Provide the child’s birth certificate.
    

#### Documents

*   Mandatory supporting documents for Kenyan citizens and Non Kenyan residents:
    
    *   Original / copy of National ID or valid passport of parent or legal guardian opening account on behalf of minor.
    *   Copy / Original Birth Certificate of minor
    *   In-Branch: One passport size photograph
    *   If US Person, upload W-8 BEN / W-9 BEN form.
    *   If US Green Card holder, upload W-9 BEN.

## FAQs

*    What are the fees and charges on the Savings Account?
    
    There are no monthly fees or charges on this account.
    
*    What interest rates do you offer on this product?
    
    The interest rate offered on your savings account will vary from time to time subject to the applicable law. The larger your savings balance, the more interest you earn.
    
*    How many times can you transact on your Savings Account?
    
    You can only transact once per month.
