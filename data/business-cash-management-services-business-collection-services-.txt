## Business Collection Services – Standard Chartered Kenya

Our collections solution leverages the Bank's extensive branch network across our key markets to specially tailor solutions for your collection needs.

## Our Solution

A solution delivered through a standardised international platform with the flexibility of catering to your local needs, thus enabling you to meet your objectives of reducing costs and increasing efficiency.

*   ### Timely receivables

    Ensuring receivables are collected in an efficient and timely manner to optimize utilization of your funds.
*   ### Effective risk management

    Ensuring effective management of debtors to eliminate risk of returns and losses caused by defaulters and delayed payments.
*   ### Quick Inventory turnaround

    Ensuring efficient and quick turnaround of inventory to maximize returns.
*   ### We provide collection services for:

    *   Cash Pick Up /Delivery
    *   E-Commerce Services
    *   Local/Foreign currency cheques
    *   Cash Depositor Machines
    *   Tailored Paybill Numbers
    *   PDQ/POS Machines.
