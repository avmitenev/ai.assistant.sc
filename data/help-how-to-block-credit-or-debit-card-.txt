## How to block credit or debit card

**How to block credit or debit card**

**STEP 1**

Login to the App

**STEP 2**

Select “Service Requests”

**STEP 3**

Go to “Card Management”

Select “Block & Replace Debit Card” if blocking a debit card

Select “Report Lost/Stolen Credit Card” if blocking a credit card

**STEP 4**

Enter “Reason to Block” and select the card to be blocked

If replacing, input the address that new card will be delivered to
